# Expansion of Deforestation in the Maranhão Cerrado (2001–2023)

This repository contains the spatial analysis and datasets used in the study: 
**"EXPANSION OF DEFORESTATION IN THE CERRADO (BRAZILIAN SAVANNA) OF MARANHÃO STATE, BRAZIL (2001-2023)"**.

Using PRODES data, this research examines the temporal evolution of deforestation across different territorial divisions in Maranhão. The analysis highlights a significant increase in deforestation since 2020 and identifies key regions under pressure, such as Balsas and the microregions of Presidente Dutra, Caxias, and Chapadinha.

## Methodology & Data
The study utilizes a regular grid analysis to eliminate municipal area bias, providing a clearer visual representation of how deforestation has spread across the Maranhão Cerrado over the last 22 years.

* **Data Source:** [PRODES (INPE)](http://www.obt.inpe.br/obt/assuntos/programas/amazonia/prodes)
* **Key Metrics:** Absolute and relative deforestation, microregional variation, and municipal rankings.
